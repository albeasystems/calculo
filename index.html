FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is the app free? ▼
Yes. All four calculators (loans, deposits, prepayment and capital) are completely free. The only optional purchase ($5.99 one-time) is to permanently remove ads.
Do I need to create an account? ▼
No. Capital works without registration, without an account and without an internet connection. Open the app and start calculating immediately. We don't collect any personal data.
How do I calculate an early repayment on my mortgage? ▼
Go to the Prepayment section, enter your outstanding mortgage balance, interest rate, remaining term and the lump-sum amount you want to repay early. The app will instantly show you how much you save in interest and how much the term or monthly payment is reduced.
Can I see the full amortization schedule? ▼
Yes. From the loan calculator you can access the full monthly amortization table, where you'll see for each period the payment amount, interest paid, principal repaid and outstanding balance.
What's the difference between reducing the payment and reducing the term? ▼
When making a prepayment you can choose to reduce the monthly payment (keeping the original term but paying less each month) or reduce the term (keeping the payment but finishing sooner). The calculator shows the interest savings for both options.
Is it available for Android? ▼
Capital is currently available for iPhone only (iOS 16+). We are evaluating Android support for future versions.
I paid to remove ads but they're still showing. ▼
Go to Settings → Restore Purchase inside the app. This will verify your purchase with Apple and remove all ads. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used when purchasing.
